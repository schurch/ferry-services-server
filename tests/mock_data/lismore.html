Supplementary information

Thursday 9 June - Saturday 11 June

Due to an essential repair to MV Loch Striven, MV Raasay will operate this service from 06:50 on Thursday 9 June until 12:00 on Saturday 11 June inclusive. 

MV Raasay cannot carry as many vehicles as MV Loch Striven so bookings for vehicles during this period is essential. There will be no disruption to timetable.


We are operating an amended timetable due to tidal conditions throughout the summer period.

You can find full details of the timetable on our website here - 

https://www.calmac.co.uk/service-changes/oban-lismore-tidal-amendments

Sunday 20 March - Monday 8 August

Train services to Oban will be affected by Queen st tunnel works.

Customers intending to travel please see https://www.scotrail.co.uk/queenstreettunnel for more information and timetables.
